Privacy Policy for Glucostats

Last updated: 26.03.2025

1. Information We Collect

a. Health Data

The App allows you to track sensitive health information, including glucose levels, insulin doses, nutrition, weight, HbA1c, and blood pressure.

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party. We do not access, process, or share your personal health data.

b. Google Sign-In

Signing in with Google is optional. If you sign in, your email address is used only for analytics purposes and is stored in Google Analytics to help us understand app usage patterns.

c. Usage Analytics

We use the following services to understand how users interact with our app and to improve the experience:

  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These tools may collect anonymized data such as:

  • Events (button clicks, screens viewed)
  • Device type and OS
  • General location (country-level)
  • Email (if signed in with Google)

We do not collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • User IDs
  • IP addresses
  • Session tracking or behavioral profiling

2. Future Use of Cloud Services and AI

We plan to introduce features in the future that may:

  • Back up your data to a secure cloud server (hosted in Germany)
  • Provide personalized suggestions or insights based on your logged data
  • Send push notifications (e.g., reminders or insights)

When these features are introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be informed before any changes take effect.

3. No Advertising

We do not serve ads and do not use any advertising SDKs such as AdMob.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store data from children under 13 years of age.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information. All personal health data is kept locally and is not transmitted over the internet.

6. Legal Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Germany, where the app is developed and hosted.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
